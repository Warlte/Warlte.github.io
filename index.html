But first let’s discuss how this problem affects people and the world around us. It has a very large impact on people living in poorer countries since a large amount of the richer countries' garbage ends up being dumped there creating these large mountains of garbage. This has a negative effect on the people living there in their health and creates an unsafe environment for those living nearby.

Recycling

A very simple solution in theory is to just recycle a lot more than we already do since that would mean less trash in landfills and allows us to reuse resources. Although not all countries have the necessary infrastructure to allow for this unfortunately.

Biodegradable

One of the big problems with trash is that a lot of it just does not decompose at all so it just sits wherever it was dumped forever and causes harm. One of the most common forms of non degradable trash is plastic. But there have been efforts in order to make biodegradable plastic bottles with a halflife of 18 months.

Plasma

A future solution is plasma gasification which is when you pass electricity through two electrodes which produces a temperature higher than the surface of the sun in a no oxygen environment. This does not burn the trash though since it takes place in a zero oxygen environment and instead breaks down the molecular bonds making the trash into gas or slag. The gas can in turn be used to power the plant.
